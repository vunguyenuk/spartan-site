The problem

🔻AI calorie estimation is happening outside nutrition apps. Many users already ask ChatGPT to estimate calories from meals, but general AI is not built for daily tracking.

🔻The market is shifting from tracking to coaching. The winning product is not the one that only records food. It is the one that turns daily nutrition data into a clear loop: log + understand + adjust + repeat.

🔻Speed has become the new expectation. Users no longer accept slow food search and manual entry.

👉🏻DietFit is the missing intelligence layer between food tracking, real-time guidance, and behavior change.
