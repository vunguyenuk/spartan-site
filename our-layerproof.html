Creative system with multiple sub-products

The problem

🔻Creative work is fragmented by default. Copy lives in one doc, design in another, brand assets somewhere else. Nothing connects, so on-brand output requires manual coordination every single time.

🔻Polishing visuals steals time. A few hours per team is spent reformatting social content — and fixing decks before they’re presentable.

🔻AI kills consistency. As AI visual output grows, brand voice drifts, visual quality varies, and approvals pile up. AI raises speed and quality become a burden.

👉🏻The bottleneck is a missing creative operating system that can bridge source inputs to finished assets.

How it works

LayerProof is a creative system powered by tailored AI image generation models.
